The United Empire (UE) is a community of players in Rise of Kingdoms specializing in the jump process. We welcome players of all skill levels and accept both Free-to-Play and Pay-to-Win players. Our goal is to be an active and supportive community, aiding each other in growth and success within the game. We are constantly looking for new members to join our realm.
